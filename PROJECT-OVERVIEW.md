## 🚀 Quick Start (3 Steps)

### 1️⃣ Customize (5 minutes)
Open `index.html` and change:
- Your names (line 30-31)
- Wedding date (line 35)
- Venue info (lines 65-70)
- Schedule (lines 78-110)

Open `app.js` and change:
- Wedding date for countdown (line 4)

### 2️⃣ Deploy (2 minutes)
1. Go to [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
2. Drag your `InviteYes` folder
3. Customize the URL
4. Done!

### 3️⃣ Share (1 minute)
Send your URL via WhatsApp:
```
🎉 You're invited! View details & RSVP:
https://your-site.netlify.app
```
